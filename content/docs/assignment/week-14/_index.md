# Week 14 - Molding and Casting

Week 14 focused on **molding and casting**, learning to create molds and cast parts using various materials.  
The aim was to understand mold design, material properties, and the casting process for reproduction of parts.

This week introduced replication techniques that complement the additive and subtractive processes learned earlier.

---

## Group Assignment
- Review the **safety data sheets** for each of your molding and casting materials
- Make and compare **test casts** with each of them
- Compare **mold making processes**

## Individual Assignment
- Design a mold around the process you'll be using
- Produce it with a **smooth surface finish** that does not show the production process toolpath
- Use it to **cast parts**

**Extra Credit Goals**
- Use **more than two mold parts**
- Try **different casting materials**
